How to delete your account — Expense Book

Follow these steps in your mobile app.

Steps

  1. 1
    Go to the Settings page on the app
    Click on the hamburger icon displayed on right top of Home Page Settings.
  2. 2
    Click on Delete account
    You may be asked to confirm your identity (PIN / password) depending on your app settings.
  3. 3
    After confirmation you will see:
    "Account deleted successfully" and you will be logged out.

What will be deleted?

Everything will be deleted, including:

  • All transactions and records
  • Username and profile information
  • Email and login information
  • Any linked settings or preferences
